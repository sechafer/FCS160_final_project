    Main Challenge Faced

    ‚ö†Ô∏è

    Fast Food Temptation During Stressful Periods

    During the second week, we faced work and study pressure, which tempted us to buy fast food for convenience instead of following our meal plan.

    üß† Why This Was Our Biggest Challenge

    Stress reduces willpower and decision-making capacity
    Fast food provides immediate convenience during busy times
    Old habits resurface under pressure
    Time constraints made meal prep seem impossible
    12

    How We Overcame the Challenge

    üí° Five-Strategy Solution

    1. Advanced Preparation
    Cooked easy-to-heat emergency meals in advance
    2. Healthy Snack Strategy
    Always kept fruits and nuts readily available
    3. Communication System
    Created mutual reminder system when one felt tempted
    4. Controlled Flexibility
    In emergencies, choose the healthiest available option
    5. Visual Motivation
    Put photos of our health goals on the refrigerator
    Result: Successfully navigated stress without abandoning our plan
    13

    Project Evaluation: What I'd Change (1/2)

    1. More Detailed Nutritional Planning

    Specific change: Include detailed nutritional planning with calorie and macronutrient calculations from the beginning, not just in the third week.

    2. Emergency Situation Preparation

    Specific change: Create from week one a list of 10 quick and healthy meal options that could be prepared in less than 15 minutes.

    3. Enhanced External Support

    Specific change: Include bi-weekly meetings with Maria (our mentor) for continuous feedback and plan adjustments, instead of just one initial interview.
    14

    Project Evaluation: What I'd Change (2/2)

    4. Implement Reward System

    Specific change: Establish small non-food rewards for each successfully completed week (like a special movie or a walk together).

    5. Richer Documentation Process

    Specific change: Besides the food journal, include photos of meals and notes about how we felt after each meal, creating a more complete record.

    üéØ Overall Improvement Strategy

    These changes would create a more comprehensive, supportive, and sustainable approach to healthy eating transformation.
